Danni Dikes is a multimedia journalist in Miami, Florida. Originally from Weston, Florida, she fell in love with the arts at a young age. Danni performed in musicals and plays from Pre-K through high school and first ventured into the world of television during her time at Stagedoor Manor. After auditioning for conservatory programs across the country, she decided to attend the University of Miami to pursue a degree in both Theatre Arts and Broadcast Journalism.

In the past three years at UM, Danni has covered the 2016 Presidential Election, the November Paris attacks, the anti-LGBT laws in North Carolina, the inaugural ceremony of UM President Julio Frenk and the sudden death of a fellow student at the University of Miami. Danni served for two years as a Reporter, Entertainment Host, and Sportscaster for UMTV Newsbreak, a daily 90-second update designed for the web. She also worked as a Reporter and Associate Producer of Newsvision, UMTV’s award-winning live show. Danni will serve as Executive Producer for UMTV Newsvision in her Senior Year at the University of Miami.

Outside of the studio, Danni enjoys her time as Director of Philanthropy and Dance Choreographer for her sorority, Delta Gamma. She has interned with SFL-CW, and CBS4 Neighbors4Neighbors throughout her college career. Danni has experience with pre and post-production, graphic design, web design and social media.
